Party Guidelines

CossiePlay party-goers party at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety. Party fees must be paid at the door and cash or EFTPOS will be accepted on the night. Though, credit card payments will extract a 2% surcharge. Alcohol is served at the bar to over 18s only though no intoxicated persons will be served, but will be escorted to a taxi – paid by the party-goer.
